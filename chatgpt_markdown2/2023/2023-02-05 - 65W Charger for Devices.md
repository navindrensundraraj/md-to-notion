# 65W Charger for Devices

---
**User:**

Can I use a 65 watt charger for devices tht support less than 65 watt charging?

---
**Assistant:**

Yes, you can use a 65 watt charger for devices that support less charging, but the charging speed may be slower than with a charger specifically designed for the device. It's recommended to use a charger that's specifically designed for the device or one that supports the same wattage or less.
