# Headache Fever Early Pregnancy

---
**User:**

Can someone get sudden headache or fever during early conception

---
**Assistant:**

Yes, some people may experience mild headaches or a slight increase in body temperature during early conception due to hormonal changes. Here’s why:  

1. **Hormonal Changes** – Increased progesterone levels can cause body temperature to rise slightly (known as basal body temperature rise) and may contribute to headaches.  
2. **Increased Blood Flow** – Early pregnancy leads to increased blood circulation, which can trigger headaches.  
3. **Implantation Symptoms** – Some women experience mild feverish feelings or discomfort when the fertilized egg implants into the uterus.  
4. **Immune System Changes** – Pregnancy weakens the immune response slightly, which may cause mild fever-like symptoms.  

However, if the fever is above **100.4°F (38°C)** or is accompanied by severe symptoms, it may indicate an infection rather than normal pregnancy changes. If concerned, it’s best to consult a doctor.
