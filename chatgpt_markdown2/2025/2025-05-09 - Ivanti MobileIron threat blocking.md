# Ivanti MobileIron threat blocking

---
**User:**

Ivanti mobileiron app keeps blocking threat on my phone

---
**Assistant:**

If the Ivanti MobileIron app keeps blocking threats on your phone, it likely means it's detecting something it considers suspicious or non-compliant with your organization's mobile security policies. Here's what you can do:

### 1. **Review the Threat Details**
- Open the Ivanti Mobile@Work app.
- Go to the **Threat Defense** or **Security** section.
- Tap on the notification or alert to view the **details** of what’s being blocked (e.g., app name, URL, network behavior).

### 2. **Common Causes**
- **Suspicious Apps**: Some third-party apps (especially sideloaded or from unknown sources) might be flagged.
- **Unsecured Wi-Fi**: If you're connected to a public or poorly encrypted Wi-Fi network.
- **Outdated OS or Apps**: Some security policies may require the latest version of your OS or certain apps.
- **Jailbroken/Rooted Devices**: These are often flagged as high-risk.

### 3. **Actions You Can Take**
- **Update your OS and apps** from official sources.
- **Uninstall flagged apps** or move off suspicious networks.
- **Contact your IT support** if this is a work-managed device—they can whitelist safe apps or explain the policy enforcement.
- **Temporarily disable Wi-Fi/VPN** to check if the alerts are tied to your connection.
