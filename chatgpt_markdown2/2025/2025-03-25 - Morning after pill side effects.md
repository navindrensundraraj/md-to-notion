# Morning after pill side effects

---
**User:**

Can morning after pill cause throat pain

---
**Assistant:**

The morning-after pill (emergency contraception) is not commonly associated with throat pain as a direct side effect. However, there are a few possible reasons why you might experience throat discomfort after taking it:  

1. **Acid Reflux or Nausea** – The pill can cause nausea in some people, leading to acid reflux, which may irritate your throat.  
2. **Mild Allergic Reaction** – If you have a sensitivity to any ingredient in the pill, it could cause throat irritation.  
3. **Dryness or Dehydration** – Hormonal changes can sometimes lead to mild dehydration, which might cause a dry or sore throat.  
4. **Coincidental Illness** – You may have an unrelated infection (like a cold or flu) that started around the same time.  

If the throat pain is severe, persists for more than a few days, or is accompanied by difficulty breathing or swallowing, you should seek medical attention.
