# Towel sperm pregnancy risk

---
**User:**

I used my gf towel to wipe sperm. Will it get her pregnant?

---
**Assistant:**

No, your girlfriend **cannot get pregnant from using a towel that has sperm on it**, especially if:

- The sperm had already dried (sperm die quickly when exposed to air).
- She did not use the towel internally (e.g., inside the vagina).
- There was a time delay between when you used it and when she used it.

**Pregnancy requires live sperm to be deposited inside the vagina**, typically through ejaculation during intercourse. Sperm exposed to air, water, or fabric dies within minutes and is no longer viable.

So unless you ejaculated directly onto the towel and she used it immediately and internally, **there is virtually no risk of pregnancy**.
