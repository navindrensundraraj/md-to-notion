# Soya Milk After Spicy Food

---
**User:**

Does soya milk help after spicy food

---
**Assistant:**

Yes, **soya milk can help soothe the burning sensation** after eating spicy food. Here's why:  

1. **Fat Content** – While lower than dairy milk, soya milk still contains some fat, which can help dissolve capsaicin (the compound responsible for spiciness).  
2. **Protein** – The proteins in soya milk can bind to capsaicin and reduce its irritation.  
3. **Cooling Effect** – The liquid itself provides immediate relief by washing away some of the spice from your mouth.  

However, **dairy milk is generally more effective** because it has higher fat and casein protein content, which neutralizes capsaicin better. But if you're lactose intolerant or prefer plant-based options, soya milk is a good alternative.
