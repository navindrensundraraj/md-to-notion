# Mead Taste Overview

What does mead taste like

---

Mead tastes like a blend of **honey and wine**, but the exact flavor can vary a lot depending on how it's made. Here's a general idea:

- **Sweetness**: Most meads have a noticeable honey sweetness, but it can range from **dry to very sweet**.
- **Alcohol warmth**: Like wine, mead can have a warm, boozy kick (usually around 6–14% ABV).
- **Floral and fruity notes**: Since it's made from honey, you might taste **floral undertones**. Some meads are infused with fruits, herbs, or spices, which adds flavors like **berries, citrus, cinnamon, or ginger**.
- **Body and texture**: It can be **light and crisp** or **rich and syrupy**, depending on style.
