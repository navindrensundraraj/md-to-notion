- Some brands have **28 active pills** per pack, and you start a new pack immediately after finishing one.  

### **How Long Should You Take It?**  
- You can take the pill as long as you need contraception.  
- Some people take it continuously for years, while others stop when planning pregnancy or if they experience side effects.

Abortion is generally **safe** when performed correctly, but like any medical procedure, there are potential risks and complications. The risks depend on the **type of abortion** and **how far along the pregnancy is**.  

---

### **1. Medical Abortion (Abortion Pills) – Up to ~10 Weeks**  
Uses **Mifepristone + Misoprostol** or just **Misoprostol** to induce a miscarriage.  

#### **Possible Complications:**  
- **Heavy Bleeding** – Some bleeding is normal, but **soaking more than 2 pads per hour for 2+ hours** could indicate excessive bleeding.  
- **Severe Cramps & Pain** – Stronger than period cramps, but extreme pain could signal a problem.  
- **Incomplete Abortion** – If not all pregnancy tissue is expelled, a surgical procedure may be needed.  
- **Infection** – Rare, but signs include fever, chills, foul-smelling discharge.  
- **Nausea & Diarrhea** – Temporary side effects from misoprostol.  

---

### **2. Surgical Abortion (Vacuum Aspiration or Dilation & Evacuation) – Up to ~24 Weeks**  
A doctor removes the pregnancy tissue using suction (vacuum aspiration) or instruments (D&E for later pregnancies).  

#### **Possible Complications:**  
- **Infection** – Symptoms include fever, severe pain, and foul-smelling discharge.  
- **Excessive Bleeding** – Rare but can occur.  
- **Perforation of the Uterus** – Very rare but possible if instruments accidentally puncture the uterus.  
- **Cervical Damage** – The cervix is gently opened, but in rare cases, it may get injured.  
- **Scar Tissue Formation (Asherman’s Syndrome, Rare)** – Can cause menstrual irregularities and fertility issues.  

---

### **Long-Term Effects & Emotional Considerations**  
- **Future Fertility** – If done safely, abortion does **not** affect future pregnancy chances.
