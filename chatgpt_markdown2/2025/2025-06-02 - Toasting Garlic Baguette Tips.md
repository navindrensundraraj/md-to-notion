# Toasting Garlic Baguette Tips

---
**User:**

How long should i toast french baguette with garlic spread on it

---
**Assistant:**

To toast a French baguette with garlic spread, follow this guide:

### 🔥 **Oven Method (Best for Even Crisping)**
1. **Preheat** your oven to **180–200°C (350–400°F)**.
2. **Slice** the baguette diagonally into 1–2 cm (½–¾ inch) thick slices.
3. **Spread** your garlic butter or garlic spread generously on each slice.
4. **Place** slices on a baking tray (cut side up).
5. **Toast** for **8–12 minutes**, or until:
   - The edges are golden brown
   - The garlic spread is bubbly

For **extra crisp**, broil for the last **1–2 minutes**, but watch closely to avoid burning.

---

### 🔥 **Air Fryer Method**
1. Set air fryer to **180°C (350°F)**.
2. Toast for **4–6 minutes**, checking for crispness halfway.

---

### 🔥 **Toaster Oven or Grill (Small Batch)**
- Toast at medium-high heat for **5–8 minutes**.
