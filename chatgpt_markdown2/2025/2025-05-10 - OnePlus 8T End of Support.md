### **Issues You May Face with LineageOS:**

#### 1. **Banking Apps / E-Wallets**
- Many apps check for **Google Play Integrity / SafetyNet**, which LineageOS **fails by default**.
- You might not be able to open or use some banking apps.
- Workarounds like **Magisk + Zygisk + Play Integrity Fix** exist, but:
  - They require root access (which lowers security if not carefully managed).
  - They are technical and may break with future updates.
  - You’ll need to constantly monitor to ensure the apps still work.

#### 2. **Netflix and Other DRM Services**
- On LineageOS, **Netflix may be limited to SD quality** because:
  - It lacks **Widevine L1** certification after bootloader unlock.
  - You’ll get Widevine L3 (basic level), which only allows SD streaming.
- Fixing this usually isn’t possible due to hardware-bound security.

#### 3. **Google Services**
- If you want full Google services (Play Store, location accuracy, push notifications), you’ll need to flash **GApps** separately.
- Still, Play Integrity checks may fail, even with GApps.

---

### **So, Is It Worth It?**
**Probably not**, *unless*:
- You’re comfortable with rooting, patching, and fixing things when they break.
- You prioritize privacy and control over absolute convenience and don't mind losing HD Netflix or risking temporary banking app issues.
