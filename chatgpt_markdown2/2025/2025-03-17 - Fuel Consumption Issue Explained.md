# Fuel Consumption Issue Explained

---
**User:**

Better avg consumption but fuel runs out faster

---
**Assistant:**

If your Proton Saga's fuel consumption *appears* better but the fuel runs out faster, a few factors could be at play:  

### Possible Reasons:  
1. **Fuel Gauge Inaccuracy** – The fuel level sensor might not be reading accurately, making it seem like the fuel depletes faster.  
2. **Fuel Tank Not Filling Up Completely** – If there's a venting issue or you stop refueling early, the tank might not be at full capacity.  
3. **Different Driving Conditions** – If your driving habits changed (e.g., more highway driving), the displayed fuel economy might improve, but total fuel use could still be high.  
4. **Calculation Errors** – If you’re relying on the car’s trip meter rather than a full-tank method (mileage vs. liters used), the average could be misleading.  
5. **Fuel Quality or Density Changes** – Different fuel stations or fuel types can affect mileage, and temperature changes can alter fuel density.  

### What You Can Do:  
- **Manually track fuel consumption** by noting the liters filled and distance traveled over multiple refuels.  
- **Check for fuel leaks** (though unlikely, it's worth inspecting).  
- **Observe driving patterns**—are you accelerating harder or driving in a different environment?  
- **Try a different fuel station** to rule out bad fuel issues.  

Are you seeing this change suddenly, or has it been a gradual trend?
