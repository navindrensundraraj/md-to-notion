# Time Calculation for Videos

---

---

To calculate how long **1.1k (1,100) times 20 minutes per video** is:

\[
1,100 \times 20 \text{ minutes} = 22,000 \text{ minutes}
\]

Now convert it to **hours and days**:

- **Hours**:  
  \[
  \frac{22,000}{60} = 366.67 \text{ hours}
  \]

- **Days** (assuming 24 hours/day):  
  \[
  \frac{366.67}{24} \approx 15.28 \text{ days}
  \]

### Final Answer:
- **22,000 minutes**
- **≈ 367 hours**
- **≈ 15.3 full days (nonstop)**
