Here are some great podcasts that explore the daily routines and habits of highly successful people:  

1. **The Tim Ferriss Show** – Tim Ferriss interviews top performers (athletes, CEOs, artists) to deconstruct their habits, routines, and productivity strategies.  

2. **The Daily Stoic Podcast** – Hosted by Ryan Holiday, this podcast explores daily habits inspired by Stoic philosophy and interviews successful individuals on their routines.  

3. **The Life Coach School Podcast** – Brooke Castillo shares insights on mindset, time management, and daily habits of high achievers.  

4. **The Tony Robbins Podcast** – Tony Robbins discusses peak performance, business success, and daily routines with elite professionals.  

5. **The School of Greatness** – Hosted by Lewis Howes, this podcast features successful people sharing their morning routines, productivity tips, and mindset strategies.  

6. **The Knowledge Project** – Shane Parrish interviews thought leaders to uncover decision-making frameworks and daily habits for success.  

7. **The High Performance Podcast** – Hosted by Jake Humphrey and Prof. Damian Hughes, this podcast explores habits and routines of top athletes, CEOs, and entrepreneurs.
