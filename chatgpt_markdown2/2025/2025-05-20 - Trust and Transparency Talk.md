# Trust and Transparency Talk

---
**User:**

My girlfriend keeps thinking I'm chatting with another girl but I'm actually chatting with my work group

---
**Assistant:**

It sounds like your girlfriend is feeling insecure or suspicious, even though you're being honest. Here’s how you can approach it:

1. **Reassure her calmly** – Let her know that you understand how she feels and that you have nothing to hide. Say something like, *“I get why you might feel that way sometimes, but I promise it’s just work stuff.”*

2. **Be transparent** – Without sounding defensive, offer to show her the chat (if you're comfortable). This can help build trust: *“If you want, I can show you—it’s really just the team talking about our tasks.”*

3. **Ask what's behind her worry** – Gently ask if there’s something deeper bothering her: *“Is there something that’s been making you feel insecure lately?”* Listen without judgment.

4. **Set healthy boundaries** – It’s okay to have privacy and space. But if this happens often, a conversation about trust and communication is needed.

Do you think she’s acting this way because of something that happened before (maybe in past relationships or between you two)?
