# Tuna egg mayo shelf life

---
**User:**

How long can tuna egg mayo sandwich last if i refrigerate it

---
**Assistant:**

A **tuna egg mayo sandwich** can last safely in the refrigerator for about **3 days**, assuming it’s stored properly:

### Storage Tips:
- **Refrigerate within 2 hours** of making it.
- Keep it in an **airtight container** or **wrap tightly** in cling film or foil.
- Store it at or below **4°C (40°F)**.

### Signs it's gone bad:
- Sour smell
- Slimy texture
- Discoloration

For best taste and safety, eat it **within 1–2 days**. The combination of tuna, egg, and mayo is prone to bacterial growth if not kept cold.
