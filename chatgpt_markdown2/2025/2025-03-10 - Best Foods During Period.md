### **2. Magnesium-Rich Foods** (to help with cramps)

- Cucumbers, watermelon  
- Oranges, berries  
- Herbal teas (chamomile, ginger, peppermint)  

### **5. Complex Carbohydrates** (for steady energy and better mood)  
- Whole grains (brown rice, oats, quinoa)  
- Sweet potatoes  
- Lentils, chickpeas  

### **6. Foods with Vitamin B6** (to help with mood swings)  
- Bananas  
- Potatoes  
- Eggs  

### **Foods to Avoid**  
- **Caffeine & Sugary Drinks:** Can worsen cramps and bloating  
- **Salty Foods:** Can increase water retention  
- **Processed & Fried Foods:** Can cause inflammation
