Pizza Hut's mushroom soup is a creamy, savory dish that's smooth with a rich mushroom flavor—often served as a starter in many Asian branches. While the exact recipe is proprietary, you can replicate a very similar version at home. Here's a close imitation of Pizza Hut-style creamy mushroom soup:

### **Copycat Pizza Hut Mushroom Soup Recipe**

**Ingredients:**
- 200g fresh button mushrooms (sliced thinly)
- 1 small onion (finely chopped)
- 2 cloves garlic (minced)
- 2 tbsp butter
- 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
- 2 cups chicken or vegetable broth
- 1 cup full cream milk or evaporated milk
- 1/2 cup heavy cream (optional for extra creaminess)
- Salt and black pepper to taste
- 1 tsp chicken stock powder (for that MSG-style umami boost, optional)
- A pinch of white pepper (optional, closer to restaurant flavor)

**Instructions:**
1. **Sauté Aromatics**: In a pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Add onions and garlic, sauté until translucent.
2. **Cook Mushrooms**: Add sliced mushrooms and cook until soft and slightly browned, about 5-7 minutes.
3. **Add Flour**: Sprinkle flour over the mushrooms and stir well to form a roux. Cook for 1-2 minutes to remove raw flour taste.
4. **Add Liquids**: Slowly pour in the broth while stirring to prevent lumps. Then add milk. Bring to a gentle simmer.
5. **Blend (optional)**: For a smoother texture like Pizza Hut’s, use an immersion blender or transfer to a blender to blend until smooth.
6. **Simmer & Season**: Return to heat, add salt, black pepper, white pepper, and chicken stock powder. Simmer for 5-10 minutes until thickened.
7. **Finish**: Stir in heavy cream (optional), simmer for 2 more minutes, then serve hot with garlic bread.
