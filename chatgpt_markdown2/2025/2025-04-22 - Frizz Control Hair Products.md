Does coconut oil work?

**Assistant:**

Yes, **coconut oil can work really well**—but it depends on your hair type and how you use it.

### **Benefits of Coconut Oil for Hair:**
- **Deeply moisturizes:** It penetrates the hair shaft better than most oils.
- **Reduces protein loss:** Great if your hair is damaged or chemically treated.
- **Tames frizz & adds shine:** Especially useful for dry, frizzy hair.
- **Helps with detangling:** Makes combing easier when applied lightly.

---

### **How to Use Coconut Oil:**

**1. As a Pre-Wash Treatment (Best Way)**
- Apply a small amount to dry hair 30–60 mins before shampooing.
- Focus on mid-lengths to ends.
- Shampoo and condition as usual.
- Result: Softer, less frizzy hair after washing.

**2. As a Leave-In (Use Sparingly)**
- Take a pea-sized amount, warm it in your palms, and lightly apply to damp hair ends.
- Avoid roots—can make hair look greasy.
- Works well to tame flyaways or add shine.

---

### **Who Should Use It:**
- **Best for:** Thick, coarse, curly, or very dry hair.
- **Caution for:** Fine or oily-prone hair—it can be too heavy and make it greasy.

---

If you want a lighter alternative, **argan oil or jojoba oil** might suit better—less greasy but still moisturizing.
