# Brownies and Kunafa Vegetarian_

Whether **brownies** and **kunafa** are vegetarian depends on the specific ingredients used, but here's a general breakdown:

---

### 🟤 **Brownies**  
**Usually vegetarian**, but check for:

- ✅ Common vegetarian ingredients: flour, cocoa powder, sugar, eggs, butter, vegetable oil, vanilla extract.
- ⚠️ **Eggs** are not vegan but still considered vegetarian by most vegetarians.
- ❌ Non-vegetarian risk: **Gelatin** (sometimes found in marshmallow toppings), or **animal-based rennet** in some chocolate.

✅ **Conclusion**: **Most brownies are vegetarian**, but check if toppings or chocolate used contain animal-derived additives.

---

### 🟠 **Kunafa (Knafeh)**  
**Usually vegetarian**, but watch for:

- ✅ Common vegetarian ingredients: kataifi pastry or semolina, sugar syrup, cheese (like Nabulsi or Akkawi), butter or ghee.
- ❌ **Cheese** may be made using **animal rennet**, which strict vegetarians avoid.

✅ **Conclusion**: **Kunafa is typically vegetarian**, but the **type of cheese** used is key. For strict vegetarians, look for **cheese made with microbial or plant-based rennet**.
