---
**Assistant:**

Since Amanah Saham Malaysia 3 (ASM3) operates on a fixed-price unit trust model with limited availability, your best strategy depends on:  

### **1. Securing Units (Most Critical Step)**  
- **Check Availability Regularly:** ASM3 units are limited, so monitor **Maybank2u, MyASNB, or ASNB branches** frequently.  
- **Try Multiple Banks:** Sometimes different banks show different availability.  
- **Be Ready at Midnight:** New units (if available) tend to be released late at night or early morning.  

### **2. Funding Strategy**  
- **Keep RM7,000 Ready in ASNB-Linked Bank Account** (e.g., Maybank, CIMB, RHB) to act quickly when units are available.  
- **Use Maybank2u or MyASNB** for instant transfers when you see available units.  

### **3. Portfolio Strategy (Diversification)**  
If ASM3 is unavailable or you want a diversified approach:  
- **Plan B: ASM1 or ASM2** – If available, they have the same return mechanism as ASM3.  
- **Plan C: FD-Like Alternatives** – Consider high-yield FD or **money market funds (e.g., Money Market Fund in FSMOne or StashAway Simple)** until you secure ASM3 units.  
- **Plan D: Continue DCA into VUAA/VOO** while waiting for ASM3 availability.  

### **4. Exit Strategy**  
- **ASM3 is Illiquid** – Withdrawals depend on availability, so only invest money you don’t need urgently.  
- **Reinvest Dividends:** ASM3 pays dividends yearly, which can be reinvested or redirected to other investments.

**User:**

How long does it take to withdraw from asm

---
**Assistant:**

Withdrawal from **ASM3** (or any ASNB fixed-price funds) is usually **instant** if done via:  

1. **MyASNB App / Website**  
   - Funds are credited to your bank account **within minutes to a few hours** (up to 24 hours in rare cases).  
   - Make sure your bank account is linked to ASNB for smooth transactions.  

2. **ASNB Branches & Agents (Maybank, CIMB, RHB, etc.)**  
   - Cash withdrawal at a branch is **immediate** upon request.  
   - Bank transfers may take **1 business day**.  

### **Things to Consider Before Withdrawing**  
- ASM units are hard to get back once withdrawn.  
- No penalties, but you lose future dividend potential.  
- Withdrawal can only be done during **business hours** (not 24/7 like some other investments).
