# Watch Netflix Screen Mirroring

---
**User:**

How to watch Netflix with screen mirroring

---
**Assistant:**

Netflix blocks screen mirroring on most devices due to DRM (Digital Rights Management) protection. However, here are a few workarounds depending on your setup:  

### **1. Use a Wired Connection (HDMI Adapter)**  
If your phone or tablet supports a wired connection, use:  
- **For Android**: USB-C to HDMI adapter  
- **For iPhone/iPad**: Lightning to HDMI adapter  

Once connected, select the correct HDMI input on your TV.  

### **2. Use a Smart TV or Streaming Device**  
If your TV has a built-in Netflix app, use it directly instead of mirroring. Otherwise, use a streaming device like:  
- Chromecast  
- Roku  
- Fire Stick  
- Apple TV  

These devices support Netflix natively, allowing direct streaming.  

### **3. Cast Netflix Using Chromecast**  
- Open Netflix on your phone.  
- Tap the **Cast** icon.  
- Select your Chromecast-enabled device.  

### **4. Enable Desktop Mode on a Browser (For Android Users)**  
- Open **Netflix.com** in a mobile browser (Chrome).  
- Enable **Desktop Mode** in settings.  
- Try mirroring your screen.  

This method **may not always work** due to Netflix's DRM restrictions.
