# Sun Earth Diameter Ratio

---
**User:**

What is the ratio of sun to earth diameter

---
**Assistant:**

The ratio of the Sun's diameter to Earth's diameter is approximately:

**Sun's diameter**: ~1,391,000 km  
**Earth's diameter**: ~12,742 km  

So the ratio is:

**1,391,000 / 12,742 ≈ 109**

**Answer: ~109:1**

This means the Sun is about 109 times wider than the Earth.
