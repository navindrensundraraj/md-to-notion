## 🔥 General Strategy

### Phase 1: Basic Attacks
- **Nithral uses heavy melee attacks** — dodge these, don’t block.
- He **teleports** occasionally. Watch for where he reappears and roll away.
- Use **Quen** for protection and **Igni** if you’ve upgraded it for extra damage.

### Phase 2: Ice Portal + Hounds
- At about **50% HP**, he opens a portal and becomes invulnerable.
- He summons **Wild Hunt Hounds** while regenerating.
  
🛡️ **Your goal:** Survive and kill the hounds **quickly**. Dodge their attacks and stay mobile.

🔥 **Use Aard or Igni** to knock back or kill hounds faster.

Once all hounds are dead, Nithral returns to the fight.

> ⚠️ This phase can repeat more than once depending on how much damage you do.

---

## ⚔️ Combat Tips

- **Oil Up**: Apply **Elementa Oil** on your sword to deal more damage.
- **Potions**: Use **Swallow** for healing and **Thunderbolt** for extra damage.
- **Stay Behind**: His frontal attacks are strong. Roll behind and hit him there.
- **Quen Often**: Refresh it whenever possible. It can save you from a one-hit KO if you mistime a dodge.

---

## Difficulty-Specific Tip:
On **higher difficulties** (Death March), you need to be very conservative:
- Only hit when you're sure you won’t get countered.
- Manage stamina for both **Quen** and **dodging**.
- Don’t rush the hounds — wait for one to commit, then punish.
