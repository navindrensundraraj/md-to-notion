# Monthly Savings to 1M

---
**User:**

How much do i need to contribute per month to reach 1mil ringgit with 5% avg yearly interest

---
**Assistant:**

To calculate how much you need to contribute monthly to reach RM1,000,000 with an average **5% annual return**, we can use the **Future Value of an Annuity** formula:

\[
FV = P \times \frac{(1 + r)^n - 1}{r}
\]

Where:  
- \(FV\) = RM1,000,000 (target)  
- \(P\) = monthly contribution (what we want to find)  
- \(r\) = monthly interest rate = 5% / 12 = 0.004167  
- \(n\) = number of months (years × 12)

---

### Here are a few examples based on different time horizons:

\[
1,000,000 = P \times \frac{(1 + 0.004167)^{120} - 1}{0.004167}
\Rightarrow P ≈ \text{RM} 6,440
\]

#### ✅ 15 Years (180 months):
\[
P ≈ \text{RM} 3,570
\]

#### ✅ 20 Years (240 months):

#### ✅ 25 Years (300 months):

P ≈ \text{RM} 1,400

#### ✅ 30 Years (360 months):

P ≈ \text{RM} 1,000
