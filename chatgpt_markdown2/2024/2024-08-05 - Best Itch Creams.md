The best itch cream depends on the cause of the itch and individual skin sensitivity. However, some popular over-the-counter options include:

1. **Hydrocortisone Cream:** This is a mild corticosteroid that can help reduce inflammation and itching caused by conditions like eczema, psoriasis, or allergic reactions.

2. **Antihistamine Creams:** These can be effective for allergic reactions and hives. Diphenhydramine (Benadryl) cream is a common example.

3. **Calamine Lotion:** This classic remedy helps soothe itching from poison ivy, chickenpox, and insect bites.

4. **Pramoxine Cream:** A topical anesthetic that helps numb the skin and relieve itching.

5. **Menthol or Camphor Creams:** These provide a cooling sensation that can distract from itching.

6. **Oatmeal-Based Creams:** Products containing colloidal oatmeal, like Aveeno, can be soothing for dry, irritated skin.

7. **Antifungal Creams:** If the itch is due to a fungal infection (e.g., athlete's foot), antifungal creams like clotrimazole or miconazole can be effective.

For persistent or severe itching, it's best to consult with a healthcare provider to determine the underlying cause and the most appropriate treatment.
