Aveeno offers both **Skin Relief Lotion** and **Skin Relief Cream**, which are designed to provide intense moisture and soothe dry, sensitive skin. Here’s a comparison between the two:

### **Aveeno Skin Relief Lotion**
- **Texture**: Lightweight and absorbs quickly.
- **Ingredients**: Contains colloidal oatmeal, which is known for its skin-soothing properties.
- **Use**: Suitable for daily use on the entire body. It’s good for those who prefer a lighter, non-greasy formula.

- **Texture**: Thicker and richer compared to the lotion.
- **Ingredients**: Also contains colloidal oatmeal, but in a more concentrated form, often with added emollients for intense hydration.
- **Use**: Ideal for extremely dry or compromised areas of the skin, such as elbows, knees, or hands.
- **Hydration**: Provides more intensive moisture, making it better suited for very dry or sensitive skin that needs extra care.
- **Application**: Best for targeted areas rather than full-body application due to its thicker texture.

### **Which One to Choose?**
- **For daily, all-over body use**: The **Skin Relief Lotion** is more suitable because of its lighter texture and ease of application.
- **For targeted, intense hydration**: The **Skin Relief Cream** is better, especially if you have particularly dry or rough areas that need extra care.

Both products are fragrance-free and designed for sensitive skin, so it ultimately comes down to the level of hydration you need and your preference for texture.
