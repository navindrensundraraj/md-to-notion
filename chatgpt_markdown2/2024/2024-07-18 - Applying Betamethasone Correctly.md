# Applying Betamethasone Correctly

---
**User:**

Can i apply bethanethasone cream immediately after applying moisturizer on my skin

---
**Assistant:**

It's generally recommended to apply medications like betamethasone cream on clean, dry skin for optimal absorption and effectiveness. Applying a moisturizer immediately before might create a barrier that could affect how well the betamethasone penetrates your skin.

To use both, consider this sequence:

1. Clean and dry the affected area.
2. Apply the betamethasone cream first and allow it to absorb fully.
3. After a period (usually around 15-30 minutes), you can then apply a moisturizer if needed.

For personalized advice, consult with your healthcare provider.
