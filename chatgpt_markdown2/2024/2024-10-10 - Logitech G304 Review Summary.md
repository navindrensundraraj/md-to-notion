The Logitech G304 (known as the G305 in some regions) is a popular wireless gaming mouse that delivers excellent performance at an affordable price. Here's a breakdown of its key features:

### Pros:
1. **Wireless Performance**: Uses Logitech's LIGHTSPEED wireless technology, which is known for its low-latency performance, making it virtually indistinguishable from wired connections.
2. **Battery Life**: Extremely long-lasting, with up to 250 hours of battery life on a single AA battery in performance mode, and even longer in endurance mode.
3. **Lightweight**: Weighs about 99 grams, which is relatively light for a wireless mouse, making it comfortable for extended use.
4. **HERO Sensor**: Features Logitech's HERO sensor, which is highly regarded for its accuracy and efficiency. It supports up to 12,000 DPI and offers precise tracking, even during intense gaming sessions.
5. **Build Quality**: Solid build with no creaks or flexing, and the compact design makes it suitable for various grip styles (palm, claw, or fingertip).
6. **Cost-Effective**: Offers excellent value for its price, competing with higher-end wireless gaming mice without breaking the bank.

### Cons:
1. **No Rechargeable Battery**: Uses a replaceable AA battery, which can be a downside compared to models with built-in rechargeable batteries.
2. **No RGB Lighting**: For users who value aesthetics, the G304 doesn't feature any RGB lighting, which might be a drawback for those who like customizable lighting setups.
3. **Limited Buttons**: It has 6 programmable buttons, which might not be enough for users who need more customization for MMO or productivity tasks.
4. **Small Size**: Its compact size might not be comfortable for users with larger hands, especially for palm grip users.

### Verdict:
The Logitech G304 is a fantastic wireless gaming mouse, especially for those seeking a balance between performance and price. Its lightweight design, stellar sensor, and long battery life make it a strong contender in the budget gaming mouse category. If you don’t mind using AA batteries and don't require flashy features like RGB, it’s a solid choice for gaming and general use.
