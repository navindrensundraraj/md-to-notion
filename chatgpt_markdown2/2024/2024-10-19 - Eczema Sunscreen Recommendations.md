When selecting sunscreen for eczema-prone skin, it's important to look for products that are gentle, fragrance-free, and formulated for sensitive skin. Here are some key considerations and recommendations for eczema-prone skin:

1. **Mineral-based sunscreens**: Zinc oxide and titanium dioxide are physical blockers that are less likely to irritate sensitive skin.
2. **Fragrance-free**: Fragrances can be irritating, so look for products labeled "fragrance-free" or "unscented."
3. **Hypoallergenic and Non-comedogenic**: These formulations are designed to reduce the likelihood of allergic reactions or clogged pores.
4. **Moisturizing ingredients**: Sunscreens with added moisturizers, like ceramides or glycerin, can help keep the skin hydrated, which is important for eczema-prone skin.

Some brands recommended by eczema associations or dermatologists include:
- **Neutrogena Sheer Zinc Dry-Touch Sunscreen** (Zinc oxide-based, fragrance-free)
- **La Roche-Posay Anthelios Mineral Sunscreen** (Tinted option, safe for sensitive skin)
- **Aveeno Positively Mineral Sunscreen** (Contains oat, which is soothing for eczema)
- **Blue Lizard Australian Sunscreen** (Sensitive mineral sunscreen)

Always patch-test new products and consult a dermatologist if you're unsure.
