# Washing Shoes in Machine

How to wash shoes in washing machine

Here's a guide for washing your shoes in the washing machine:

### Step 1: Check the Label
- Look for a care label on your shoes to ensure they're machine washable. Most sneakers, canvas, and fabric shoes can go in the washing machine, but suede and leather shoes should not.

### Step 2: Remove Laces and Insoles
- Take out the laces and insoles. Wash the laces separately or hand wash them, and set the insoles aside to clean by hand (to avoid damaging them in the machine).

### Step 3: Pre-Treat Stains
- If there are stubborn stains, use a gentle stain remover or a mix of water and baking soda. Scrub lightly with a soft brush, then rinse.

### Step 4: Protect Your Shoes
- Place your shoes in a mesh laundry bag or pillowcase to protect them during the wash. Adding towels can help balance the load and reduce noise.

### Step 5: Choose Settings and Detergent
- Use a gentle cycle with cold water. Add a small amount of liquid detergent (powdered detergent can get caught in crevices).
  
### Step 6: Dry Carefully
- Avoid using the dryer, as heat can warp or shrink shoes. Instead, stuff them with newspaper or paper towels to help them keep their shape, then air dry.
