# Cheesecake Vegetarian Status

---
**User:**

Is cheesecake vege

---
**Assistant:**

Cheesecake can be either vegetarian or not, depending on the ingredients used. Traditional cheesecake recipes typically contain dairy products like cream cheese, eggs, and sometimes gelatin. 

- **Cream Cheese & Eggs**: These are considered vegetarian since they come from animals but don't involve the animal's death.
- **Gelatin**: This is derived from animal collagen and is **not vegetarian**. However, many cheesecakes don't use gelatin, and there are vegetarian alternatives like agar-agar or pectin.

If you’re looking for a strictly vegetarian cheesecake, check the ingredients or ask the baker to ensure it doesn't contain gelatin or any other non-vegetarian additives.
