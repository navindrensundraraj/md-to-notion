The **GuliKit King Kong Pro 3 Max** comes in several colors, typically:

- **Black**
- **White**
- **Gray**
- **Limited Editions** (sometimes gold, green, or themed)

While the **core functionality is identical** across all colors, some **practical and aesthetic considerations** can help you choose the best one for your situation.

---

### 🔲 **1. Black**

**Advantages:**
- Hides dirt, grime, and smudges very well.
- Professional, neutral look.
- Looks new for longer over time.

**Disadvantages:**
- Shows dust and fingerprints under bright light.
- Common color – less "special" or unique.

**Best for:** Heavy users, those with sweaty hands, or someone who wants something low-maintenance.

---

### ⬜ **2. White**

**Advantages:**
- Sleek, clean, and modern Apple-like aesthetic.
- Stands out more in a gaming setup.
- Looks premium when new.

**Disadvantages:**
- Easily discolored by sweat, hand oils, or dirty environments.
- May turn yellowish over time, especially in humid climates.
- More cleaning required to keep it looking fresh.

**Best for:** Minimalist or clean setup enthusiasts who don't mind regular cleaning.

---

### 🪨 **3. Gray (or Gunmetal/Charcoal)**

**Advantages:**
- Balanced between black and white in aesthetics and dirt visibility.
- Hides light dirt/smudges better than white, looks more premium than black.
- Slightly more unique than the usual black/white options.

**Disadvantages:**
- Might not stand out as much visually.
- Still shows some fingerprints and dust.

**Best for:** People who want a mix of class, practicality, and a less-common look.

---

### 🌟 **4. Limited Editions (e.g., Gold, Green, Transparent)**

**Advantages:**
- Eye-catching and unique – often become collector’s items.
- Can match specific gaming setups (e.g., gold with Zelda themes).

**Disadvantages:**
- Usually cost more.
- May not be restocked once sold out.
- Aesthetic might not age well or suit all setups.

**Best for:** Display setups, collectors, or fans of the theme (e.g., Zelda, mecha, etc.).
