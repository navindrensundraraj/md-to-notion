# Dry Curly Hair Ingredients

**Assistant:**

Here's a breakdown of each ingredient and how it affects **dry, curly, frizzy hair**:

1. **Cyclomethicone** – A lightweight silicone that helps with detangling and adds shine without heavy buildup. Great for frizz control, especially in curly hair.

2. **Dimethicone** – A heavier silicone that forms a protective barrier, locks in moisture, and smooths frizz. Works well on dry/frizzy hair but may require occasional clarifying to prevent buildup.

3. **Argania Spinosa (Argan) Kernel Oil** – Excellent! Rich in fatty acids and vitamin E. Deeply nourishing, helps tame frizz, adds softness and shine.

4. **Fragrance** – Just a scent; doesn’t benefit or harm hair directly. Can be irritating for very sensitive scalps.

5. **Linum Usitatissimum (Linseed) Seed Extract** – Also good! Rich in omega-3s and can help strengthen hair, reduce breakage, and lock in moisture.

6. **Butylphenyl Methylpropional (Lilial)** – A fragrance ingredient. Can be sensitizing or irritating for some, especially on the scalp. Also flagged in the EU for potential safety concerns.

7. **Benzyl Benzoate** – Used as a fragrance and preservative. Generally safe in small amounts, but can be irritating for sensitive skin/scalp.

8. **Alpha-Isomethyl Ionone** – Another fragrance component. Like others, may cause sensitivity but is safe in regulated amounts.

9. **CI 47000 (Yellow 11)** – A synthetic dye. No hair care benefits; just for color. Again, can be irritating to some, but typically harmless in rinse-out products.

---

**Summary:**
- **Great ingredients for dry, frizzy, curly hair:** Argan oil, linseed extract, dimethicone, and cyclomethicone.
- **Neutral/potentially irritating:** Fragrances and dyes (especially if you have a sensitive scalp or eczema).
- **Recommendation:** If your scalp is sensitive or eczema-prone, do a patch test. But overall, this formula looks beneficial for **moisture retention, frizz control, and smoothness**.
