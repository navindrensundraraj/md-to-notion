### **Flu-Fighter Smoothie**

**Ingredients:**
- 1 ripe banana (for energy and smooth texture)  
- 1 orange (peeled, seeds removed) or ½ cup orange juice  
- ½ cup ripe papaya (cubed)  
- ½ cup water or coconut water (for hydration)  
- 1 teaspoon honey (optional — if your throat is sore)  
- A small piece of ginger (about ½ cm, optional — helps with cough and throat soothing)

**Instructions:**
1. Add everything into a blender.  
2. Blend until smooth.  
3. Drink at **room temperature** — not cold — for best comfort.

---

**Extra Tips:**  
- If your throat is sensitive, **skip the ginger**.  
- If you like, you can add a few soaked chia seeds for extra nutrients.  
- Drink slowly, in small sips.
