Here's a clear breakdown of **OLED vs Mini-LED** to help you decide which is better for your needs:

---

### 🔍 1. **Display Technology**
| Feature | OLED | Mini-LED |
|--------|------|-----------|
| **How it works** | Each pixel emits its own light (self-emissive) | Uses a backlight of many tiny LEDs behind an LCD panel |
| **Black levels** | Perfect black (pixels turn off completely) | Very good, but some light bleed may occur |
| **Contrast** | Infinite contrast | High contrast, but not as perfect as OLED |

---

### 🎨 2. **Picture Quality**
| Feature | OLED | Mini-LED |
|--------|------|----------|
| **Color accuracy** | Excellent | Excellent |
| **Brightness** | Lower peak brightness (typically 600–1000 nits) | Very bright (up to 2000+ nits) – better in bright rooms |
| **HDR performance** | Stunning in dark rooms | Brighter HDR in well-lit rooms |

---

### 🕹️ 3. **Gaming & Motion**
| Feature | OLED | Mini-LED |
|--------|------|----------|
| **Response time** | ~0.1 ms (instant) | ~2-5 ms (slightly slower) |
| **Input lag** | Very low | Also very low |
| **Burn-in risk** | Possible if static content is shown for long periods | No burn-in risk |
| **Refresh rate** | 120–240Hz | 120–240Hz |

---

### ⏳ 4. **Durability & Lifespan**
| Feature | OLED | Mini-LED |
|--------|------|----------|
| **Burn-in risk** | Yes (especially for static UI, logos) | No |
| **Overall lifespan** | Improving, but still a concern | Longer lifespan due to lack of burn-in |

---

### 💰 5. **Price**
| Feature | OLED | Mini-LED |
|--------|------|----------|
| **Generally** | More expensive for high-end models | Slightly cheaper for same screen size/specs |
| **Availability** | Common in high-end TVs and phones | Increasingly common in mid-high range monitors and TVs |

---

### 🔚 Conclusion – Which One to Choose?

| **Choose OLED if you want:** |
|-----------------------------|
- Perfect blacks & cinematic image quality  
- Best experience in **dark rooms**  
- Fastest response time for gaming  
- More immersive visuals (esp. in movies)  

| **Choose Mini-LED if you want:** |
|---------------------------------|
- Brighter screen for **daytime/bright rooms**  
- Less worry about **burn-in**  
- Good balance of performance and durability  
- Often **more affordable**
