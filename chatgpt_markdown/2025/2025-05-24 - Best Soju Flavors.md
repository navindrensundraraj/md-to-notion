For a first-time soju drinker, the **best flavors** are typically the **fruit-flavored ones**, as they're smoother, sweeter, and more approachable than the original (which has a strong alcoholic taste). Here are some top picks:

1. **Peach** – Very popular and easy to drink; sweet and fragrant.
2. **Green Grape** – Refreshing and sweet, similar to grape juice.
3. **Strawberry** – Fruity and smooth, good for beginners.
4. **Mango** – Tropical and sweet, often a crowd favorite.
5. **Yogurt (Yakult)** – Creamy, tangy-sweet, and unique—great if you like yogurt drinks.

If you're okay with something a bit stronger, you can try **Chamisul Fresh** or **Good Day Original**, which are milder versions of plain soju.
