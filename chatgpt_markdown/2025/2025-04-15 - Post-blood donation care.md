# Post-blood donation care

---
**User:**

My wife just donated blood, how to make her feel better

---
**Assistant:**

That's really thoughtful of you. After donating blood, your wife might feel a little tired or lightheaded, so here are a few things you can do to help her feel better:

### 1. **Hydrate**
Encourage her to drink plenty of water or isotonic drinks (like 100PLUS or Gatorade). This helps replenish lost fluids and maintain blood pressure.

### 2. **Feed Her Well**
Give her a light meal or snacks rich in iron and vitamin C. Some good options:
- Iron: Spinach, eggs, lean meats, beans, fortified cereals.
- Vitamin C: Oranges, guava, strawberries, or even a glass of juice.

### 3. **Let Her Rest**
Encourage her to take it easy for the rest of the day—no heavy lifting or intense activity. A nap or just lying down for a bit can help.

### 4. **Keep an Eye Out**
Watch for signs like dizziness, nausea, or fainting. If anything seems off, don't hesitate to get medical advice.

### 5. **Comfort & Treats**
A little pampering helps—a cozy blanket, some of her favorite snacks, or a sweet drink can lift her spirits. Maybe even her favorite show or movie.
