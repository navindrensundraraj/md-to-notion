# Reheat pizza in AF

How long and what temperature to reheat cold pizza using air fryer

To reheat cold pizza in an air fryer:

- **Temperature:** 160–170°C (320–340°F)  
- **Time:** 3–5 minutes

**Tips:**
- Preheat the air fryer if your model recommends it.
- Don’t overcrowd; place slices in a single layer.
- If the crust gets too hard, try placing a small piece of foil under the slice to slightly shield the bottom from direct heat.
- For extra crispiness, go closer to 170°C and 5 minutes.
