4. **Faulty Knock Sensor** – The knock sensor detects engine knock and adjusts timing accordingly. A malfunction can cause knocking to persist.  
5. **Lean Air-Fuel Mixture** – A clogged fuel injector, dirty MAF sensor, or a weak fuel pump can cause the engine to run too lean, leading to knocking.  
6. **Overheating Engine** – Excessive heat can cause pre-ignition, leading to knocking.
