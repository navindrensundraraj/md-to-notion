---

### 📈 Formula Used
We’re using the **future value of a series** formula:

\[
FV = P \times \frac{(1 + r)^n - 1}{r}
\]

Where:
- \( P = \) Annual investment (RM10,000)
- \( r = \) Annual return rate (5% or 0.05)
- \( n = \) Number of years
