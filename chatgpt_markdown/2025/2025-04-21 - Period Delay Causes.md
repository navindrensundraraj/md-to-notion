# Period Delay Causes

---

---
**Assistant:**

Period delays can happen for a variety of reasons — some common causes include:

1. **Stress** – Physical or emotional stress can affect hormone levels and disrupt your cycle.
2. **Changes in weight or diet** – Significant weight loss, gain, or eating disorders like anorexia/bulimia can cause delays.
3. **Excessive exercise** – Especially in athletes or those with very high physical activity levels.
4. **Hormonal imbalances** – Conditions like PCOS (polycystic ovary syndrome) can cause irregular or missed periods.
5. **Thyroid issues** – An overactive or underactive thyroid affects menstrual cycles.
6. **Medications** – Including birth control or emergency contraception.
7. **Pregnancy** – Always a possibility if you’re sexually active.
8. **Perimenopause** – If you're approaching your 40s or older.
9. **Illness or travel** – Especially if it affects your routine or sleep patterns.
