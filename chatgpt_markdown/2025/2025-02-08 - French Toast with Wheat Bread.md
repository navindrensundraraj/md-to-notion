# French Toast with Wheat Bread

---
**User:**

Can i make french toast using wheat bread

---
**Assistant:**

Yes, you can make French toast using wheat bread! The texture might be slightly denser compared to white bread, but it will still turn out delicious. Whole wheat bread also adds extra fiber and nutrients.  

### **Simple French Toast Recipe Using Wheat Bread**  
#### **Ingredients:**  
- 2 slices of wheat bread  
- 1 egg  
- ¼ cup milk (or non-dairy alternative)  
- ½ tsp vanilla extract (optional)  
- ½ tsp cinnamon (optional)  
- 1 tsp sugar or honey (optional)  
- Butter or oil for frying  

#### **Instructions:**  
1. In a bowl, whisk together the egg, milk, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and sugar.  
2. Heat a pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.  
3. Dip each slice of wheat bread into the mixture, ensuring both sides are coated.  
4. Place the bread on the pan and cook for 2–3 minutes on each side until golden brown.  
5. Serve with syrup, fruits, or any toppings of your choice.  

Wheat bread absorbs the egg mixture a bit more, so avoid soaking it for too long to prevent sogginess. Enjoy your healthier French toast!
