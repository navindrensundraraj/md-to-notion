# Can Women Drink Green Tea

---

Can woman drink green tea

---

Yes, women can drink green tea. It is generally safe and offers several health benefits, such as antioxidants, improved metabolism, and potential heart health support. However, there are a few considerations:  

- **Pregnancy & Breastfeeding:** Green tea contains caffeine, so pregnant and breastfeeding women should limit intake to avoid excessive caffeine consumption.  
- **Iron Absorption:** Green tea can reduce iron absorption from plant-based foods, so it’s best consumed between meals rather than with meals.  
- **Hormonal Effects:** Some studies suggest that green tea may influence estrogen levels, but the effects are usually minimal with moderate consumption.  

Drinking 1–3 cups per day is generally safe for most women.
