Early pregnancy symptoms can vary between individuals, but common signs in the first few weeks include:

1. **Missed period** – Often the first noticeable sign.
2. **Nausea or vomiting** – Commonly referred to as morning sickness, but can occur any time of day.
3. **Fatigue** – Feeling unusually tired due to hormonal changes.
4. **Breast changes** – Tender, swollen, or sore breasts; darkening of the areolas.
5. **Frequent urination** – Caused by hormonal changes and increased blood flow to the kidneys.
6. **Mood swings** – Due to hormonal fluctuations.
7. **Light spotting or implantation bleeding** – May occur when the fertilized egg attaches to the uterine lining.
8. **Food cravings or aversions** – Changes in taste and smell sensitivity.
9. **Mild cramping or bloating** – Similar to premenstrual symptoms.
10. **Constipation** – Due to slower digestion from increased progesterone levels.
