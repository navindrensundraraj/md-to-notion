# Condo Parking vs Outside

---

Since your car is brand new and you want to save money, let's break it down logically:  

### **Option 1: Paying RM80 for Condo Parking**  
✅ **Pros:**  
- **Security:** Lower risk of theft, vandalism, or accidental damage.  
- **Convenience:** No need to walk far or worry about rain/heat.  
- **Long-Term Car Condition:** Covered parking can protect against sun and rain, reducing wear on paint and interior.  

❌ **Cons:**  
- **Cost:** RM80/month = RM960/year, which could be saved or invested.  

### **Option 2: Parking Outside for Free**  
✅ **Pros:**  
- **Cost Savings:** You save RM80/month.  
- **Potentially More Flexible:** No need to worry about condo parking rules.  

❌ **Cons:**  
- **Higher Risk:** Potential theft, break-ins, scratches, or hit-and-run incidents.  
- **Weather Exposure:** Sun, rain, and extreme temperatures can damage the car over time.  
- **Inconvenience:** You may have to walk further, and finding a spot could be challenging.  

### **Final Recommendation**  
Since your car is brand new, I’d recommend **paying the RM80 for at least the first year** to keep it safe while you assess the risks of outside parking. After a year, if you notice outside parking is safe and convenient, you can reconsider.  

However, if your area has high crime or poor parking conditions, it’s probably better to **stick with the condo parking for long-term peace of mind**.
