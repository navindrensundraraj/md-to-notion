# Affordable Acne Skincare Routine

---

Best affordable skin care routine for oily and acne prone skin

---
**Assistant:**

For oily and acne-prone skin, a simple and affordable skincare routine should focus on oil control, hydration, and acne prevention. Here's a budget-friendly routine:  

### **Morning Routine**  
1. **Cleanser**: Use a gentle, foaming or gel-based face wash with **salicylic acid** (e.g., **CeraVe Foaming Cleanser**, **Simple Oil Balancing Face Wash**). This helps remove excess oil and prevent breakouts.  
2. **Toner (Optional)**: If needed, use a **niacinamide or witch hazel-based toner** (e.g., **The Ordinary Niacinamide 10% + Zinc 1%**, **Simple Soothing Facial Toner**). Niacinamide controls oil and reduces redness.  
3. **Moisturizer**: Choose a **lightweight, oil-free moisturizer** (e.g., **Neutrogena Hydro Boost Gel**, **Simple Hydrating Light Moisturizer**, **CeraVe PM Lotion**).  
4. **Sunscreen (Essential!)**: Use a **gel-based, non-comedogenic SPF 30+ sunscreen** (e.g., **Biore UV Aqua Rich**, **Eucerin Oil Control SPF 50+**). Sunscreen prevents acne scars and protects from sun damage.  

### **Night Routine**  
1. **Cleanser**: Same as the morning cleanser to remove oil and dirt.  
2. **Exfoliation (2-3 times a week)**: Use a **salicylic acid or glycolic acid toner/serum** (e.g., **The Ordinary Salicylic Acid 2% Solution**, **COSRX BHA Blackhead Power Liquid**) to unclog pores.  
3. **Treatment**: Apply a **benzoyl peroxide (2.5% or 5%) or adapalene (Differin) cream** on acne spots. This helps kill acne-causing bacteria and reduce inflammation.  
4. **Moisturizer**: Same as morning moisturizer.  

### **Extra Tips**  
- **Avoid over-washing** your face, as it can strip too much oil and cause rebound oiliness.  
- **Use blotting papers** if your skin gets too oily during the day.  
- **Drink enough water** and avoid excessive sugary foods to reduce breakouts.  
- **Change pillowcases frequently** to prevent bacteria buildup.
