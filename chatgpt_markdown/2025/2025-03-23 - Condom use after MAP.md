# Condom use after MAP

---
**User:**

Can i hv sex with her using condom after morning after pill

---
**Assistant:**

Yes, you can have sex using a condom after she has taken the morning-after pill. However, keep in mind:  

1. **Effectiveness of the Morning-After Pill** – The emergency contraceptive pill (like Plan B or ella) is not 100% effective, so using a condom ensures additional protection against pregnancy.  

2. **Ovulation Timing** – If she was already ovulating when she took the pill, it may not prevent pregnancy. Using a condom reduces the risk further.  

3. **Protection Against STIs** – A condom helps protect against sexually transmitted infections (STIs), while the morning-after pill does not.  

4. **Multiple Uses of Emergency Contraception** – The morning-after pill is not meant for regular birth control, so it's better to use consistent contraception like condoms, birth control pills, or an IUD for future encounters.  

If you have any concerns about her cycle, contraception, or STI risks, she may want to consult a healthcare provider.
