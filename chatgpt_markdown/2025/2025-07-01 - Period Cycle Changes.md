# Period Cycle Changes

A change in your period cycle can happen for many reasons, and it’s not always a cause for concern. Here are **common causes** of changes in menstrual cycles:

---

### ✅ **Normal or Common Causes:**
1. **Stress** – Affects hormone levels and ovulation.
2. **Weight changes** – Significant weight gain/loss can disrupt cycles.
3. **Exercise** – Excessive physical activity can cause missed or lighter periods.
4. **Diet** – Poor nutrition or extreme diets may impact cycles.
5. **Age**:
   - **Teens**: Irregular periods are normal in the first few years.
   - **40s**: Perimenopause can cause shorter, longer, or skipped periods.
6. **Travel or schedule changes** – Especially with jet lag or night shifts.
7. **Hormonal birth control** – Can cause lighter, skipped, or more regular periods.

---

### ⚠️ **Medical Conditions That May Cause Changes:**
- **PCOS (Polycystic Ovary Syndrome)** – Irregular or missed periods, acne, weight gain.
- **Thyroid disorders** – Both overactive and underactive thyroid can affect cycles.
- **Fibroids or endometriosis** – Heavier or painful periods.
- **Pregnancy** – Missed periods or spotting.
- **Miscarriage** – If pregnant recently, sudden heavy bleeding or cramping may occur.
