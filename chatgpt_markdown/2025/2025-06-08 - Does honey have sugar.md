# Does honey have sugar

---
**User:**

Does honey contain sugar?

---
**Assistant:**

Yes, **honey does contain sugar** — in fact, it is mostly sugar.

Here’s the typical composition of honey by weight:
- **Fructose**: ~38%
- **Glucose**: ~31%
- **Sucrose**: ~1%
- **Other sugars** (like maltose): ~9%
- **Water**: ~17%
- **Small amounts of vitamins, minerals, antioxidants, and enzymes**

So, around **80% of honey is natural sugars**, primarily **fructose and glucose**, which are simple sugars. While honey is considered more "natural" than refined sugar, it still raises blood sugar levels and should be consumed in moderation.
