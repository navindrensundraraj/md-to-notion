The **Proton Inspira** was launched in **Malaysia on November 10, 2010**, and it was essentially a rebadged **Mitsubishi Lancer** with some localized modifications.  

### **Launch Prices in Malaysia (2010):**  
1. **1.8L Manual** – **RM78,999**  
2. **1.8L CVT** – **RM84,999**  
3. **2.0L CVT** – **RM91,999**  

These prices were before insurance and road tax. The Inspira was later discontinued in 2015 when Proton introduced newer models.
