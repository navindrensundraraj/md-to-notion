# Banana ripeness and edibility

---
**User:**

Can i eat banana that's slightly brown on the outside but flesh is still white and sweet

---
**Assistant:**

Yes, you can eat a banana that has a slightly brown peel as long as the flesh inside is still white, firm, and sweet. The browning on the skin is just a sign of ripening, and the banana may even be sweeter than before. However, if the inside is mushy, has an off smell, or shows signs of mold, it's best to avoid eating it.
