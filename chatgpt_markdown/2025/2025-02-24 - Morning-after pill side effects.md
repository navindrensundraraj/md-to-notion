# Morning-after pill side effects

---

**Assistant:**

The side effects of the morning-after pill (e.g., Plan B, Postinor, Ella) typically last a few days but can vary from person to person. Here’s a general timeline for common side effects:  

### **Immediate to 1-2 Days After Taking**  
- Nausea  
- Fatigue  
- Headache  
- Breast tenderness  
- Dizziness  

### **Within a Week**  
- Abdominal pain or cramps  
- Irregular bleeding or spotting  
- Changes in mood  

### **Menstrual Cycle Changes (Up to a Month)**  
- Period may come earlier or later than usual  
- Heavier or lighter flow than normal  

Most side effects subside within **a few days to a week**. If you experience prolonged severe pain, heavy bleeding, or signs of an allergic reaction, you should consult a doctor.
