### ✅ **What to Look For in a Beginner Racquet**
- **Weight**: 80–85g (4U) – easier to control.
- **Balance**: Even or slightly head-light.
- **Flexibility**: Flexible shaft (helps generate power).
- **Durability**: Graphite or aluminum frame for casual play.
- **Pre-strung**: Saves cost unless you want custom strings.
