# Refrigerate and Reheat Pizza

---

How long can i refrigerate and reheat pizza

You can **refrigerate pizza for up to 4 days** safely, according to USDA guidelines. Here’s how to store and reheat it properly:

---

### ✅ **How to Store Pizza in the Fridge**
- **Cool it first**: Let it come to room temperature (but not more than 2 hours out).
- **Wrap properly**: Use foil, plastic wrap, or place in an airtight container.
- **Store at or below 4°C (40°F)**.

---

### 🔥 **How to Reheat Pizza**

#### **1. Air Fryer (Best for crisp crust)**
- **Temperature**: 160–180°C (320–350°F)  
- **Time**: 3–5 minutes  
- *Optional*: Line the bottom with foil or parchment for easier cleanup.

#### **2. Oven (Also great)**
- **Temperature**: 190°C (375°F)  
- **Time**: 8–10 minutes on a baking tray or pizza stone  
- *Pro Tip*: Use foil over it for softer crust; leave uncovered for crispy.

#### **3. Stovetop (Quick & crispy)**
- Heat a nonstick pan on **medium heat**
- Place pizza slice, cover with lid
- Heat for about **5 minutes** (no oil needed)

#### **4. Microwave (Soft crust)**
- **Time**: 30–60 seconds  
- **Trick**: Place a cup of water in the microwave to reduce sogginess.

---

### ⚠️ Safety Tip:
If the pizza **smells sour, has weird texture, or mold**, **do not eat it**, even if under 4 days.
