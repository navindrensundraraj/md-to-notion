The **Lenovo ThinkPad T430s**, which was released around 2012, came with several hard disk drive (HDD) and solid-state drive (SSD) options depending on the configuration. Here are the typical storage options it offered:

### Standard Hard Disk Options:

- **128GB SSD**

If you’re trying to check the **actual size** of the drive on a specific unit, you can:
- **In Windows**: Go to **This PC > Right-click on C: > Properties**.
- **In BIOS**: Press **F1** at startup to enter BIOS, then navigate to the **Storage** or **Information** tab.
- **Using Command Prompt**: Run `wmic diskdrive get size` or `diskpart` > `list disk`.
