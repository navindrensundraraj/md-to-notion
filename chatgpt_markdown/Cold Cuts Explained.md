**Cold cuts** (also called **deli meats**, **luncheon meats**, or **sliced meats**) are precooked or cured meats that are sliced and served cold or at room temperature. They're commonly used in **sandwiches**, **wraps**, or on **charcuterie boards**.

### Common Types of Cold Cuts:
- **Ham**
- **Turkey breast**
- **Roast beef**
- **Salami**
- **Bologna**
- **Mortadella**
- **Chicken breast**
- **Pastrami**

### Key Notes:
- They can be **smoked**, **salt-cured**, **air-dried**, or **cooked**.
- Some are processed (e.g., bologna), while others are more natural (e.g., sliced roast turkey).
- Often sold at **delis** or in **pre-packaged** form at grocery stores.
