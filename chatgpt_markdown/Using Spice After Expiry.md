Yes, you **can** continue using Baba's spice powders **after their "best before" date**, but with a few important considerations:

### 🔍 What "Best Before" Means:
- **"Best before" ≠ expired** — it refers to **peak quality**, not safety.
- Spice powders don't spoil like milk or meat, but they **lose potency** over time (color, aroma, and flavor fade).

---

### ✅ When It's Still Okay to Use:
- **Stored in a cool, dry, airtight container**
- **No signs of mold, clumping, or bugs**
- Smells and looks **mostly normal**, though weaker

---

### ⚠️ When to Throw It Out:
- Musty, sour, or off smell
- Visible mold or strange texture
- Insects or signs of infestation
- Totally lost color or smells like nothing

---

### 🔄 Pro Tip:
To **boost the flavor** of older spice powders:
- Lightly toast in a dry pan for a few seconds before using
- Use a bit **more than usual** to compensate for loss in strength

---
