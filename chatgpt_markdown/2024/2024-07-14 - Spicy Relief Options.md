# Spicy Relief Options

---
**User:**

Best way to relief my stomach after eating spicy food

---
**Assistant:**

Here are some effective ways to relieve a spicy stomach:

1. **Drink Milk or Eat Dairy:** Dairy products like milk, yogurt, or ice cream can help neutralize the capsaicin, the compound in spicy foods that causes the burning sensation.
   
2. **Eat Bread or Rice:** Starchy foods can help absorb some of the capsaicin and provide relief.

3. **Honey or Sugar:** A spoonful of honey or a bit of sugar can coat the stomach lining and ease the burning.

4. **Bananas:** The natural antacid properties of bananas can help soothe the stomach.

5. **Aloe Vera Juice:** This can have a cooling effect and may help reduce inflammation.

6. **Antacids:** Over-the-counter antacids can help neutralize stomach acid.

7. **Drink Plenty of Water:** Staying hydrated can help flush the spicy compounds out of your system.

Remember to avoid alcohol, caffeine, and carbonated drinks, as they can aggravate the burning sensation.
