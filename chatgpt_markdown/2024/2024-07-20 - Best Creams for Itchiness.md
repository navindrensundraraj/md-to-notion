## What causes my skin to get so itchy?

Skin can get itchy from various causes. You could have an allergic reaction to food, a bug bite, or ingredients in products that touch your skin (makeup, lotion, laundry detergent). Or you may have a skin condition like dry skin or eczema that flares up under certain conditions.

"The key is to understand what's the source of the itches because that's what's really going to help to mitigate that," says 【50†Dr. Mona Gohara,†dermatologyofct.com】 a board certified dermatologist and associate clinical professor at Yale School of Medicine. ## About Creobic Cream 

Creobic is an antifungal cream produced by Kotra Pharma, a pharmaceutical company based at Melaka, Malaysia. Fungi can be found everywhere such as in plants, soil, and even on our skin. Fungal infection in skin and nails is one of the most frequent forms of skin diseases and is proven to affect 20-25% of the population around the world. Antifungal medications work to treat fungal infection by either killing fungi directly or preventing their growth.

## Fungal Skin Infection

Fungi normally infect sweaty or damp areas that do not have sufficient airflow like feet, groin, and folds of skin. Symptoms of fungal infection differ depending on body parts but usually include rashes, discolouration of the skin, itching, scaling (shedding of skin with flakes), cracks in skin and soreness. There are a few elements that cause an increased risk of getting fungal skin infection such as living in a warm or wet environment, sweating heavily, sharing items like clothes, shoe, towels or bedding, wearing tight clothing or footwear, not keeping skin clean and dry, taking part in activities that involved frequent skin-to-skin contact, in contact with infected animals and having a weakened immune system due to immunosuppressant drugs, cancer treatment or diseases like HIV.

## Formulation And Active Ingredients

There are two common types of Creobic cream available, namely Creobic Cream and Creobic Gold. Both creams are for external use only and should be kept out of reach of children. 

### Creobic Cream Active Ingredients

The active ingredient in Creobic cream is Clotrimazole 1%w/w that mainly used to manage all fungal skin infections due to dermatophytes, yeasts, moulds, and other fungi. It is a white coloured water-miscible cream. Consult a doctor or pharmacist before starting any creobic cream, the treatment should be terminated immediately if any signs of hypersensitivity appears. 

### Creobic Gold Active Ingredients

On the other hand, Creobic Gold cream contains Terbinafine Hydrochloride 1%w/w which is fungicidal that kills fungi and moulds that cause infection. It is a water-miscible cream presented in white to off-white. It can be used to prevent the spreading of fungal infection on our face and body. It is not recommended for children. 

## Creobic Cream and Creobic Gold Benefits

Creobic Cream is used to treat all fungal infections such as ringworm infections, athlete’s foot, paronychia (inflammation of the tissue surrounding fingernail or toenail), pityriasis versicolour (yeasts infection of the skin on chest and back), erythrasma (skin infection that causes brown, scaly skin patches), intertrigo (inflammation of skin folds), fungal nappy rash, candida vulvitis (vaginal infection) and candida balanitis (penis head infection). It is used by applying the cream thinly and evenly to the affected area 2-3 times a day for at least 1 month or 2 weeks after all signs of infection disappear to prevent relapse.

Creobic Gold Cream is used for topical fungal infection caused by dermatophytes, yeasts infection, pityriasis versicolour and cutaneous candidiasis. It is recommended to apply once or twice per day and the duration of treatment varies depending on the types of infection. Consult a doctor before using it. Application of creobic gold cream should be covered with gauze strips especially at night.
