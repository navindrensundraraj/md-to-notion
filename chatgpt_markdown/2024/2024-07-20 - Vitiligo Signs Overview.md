# Vitiligo Signs Overview

---

Signs of vitiligo

---

Vitiligo is a condition characterized by the loss of skin pigmentation. Here are some common signs of vitiligo:

1. **White or light patches on the skin**: These patches can appear anywhere on the body, but are most common on sun-exposed areas like the face, hands, arms, and feet.

2. **Premature graying of hair**: Hair on the scalp, eyelashes, eyebrows, or beard may turn white or gray early.

3. **Loss of color in the mucous membranes**: This includes the inside of the mouth and nose.

4. **Loss of color in the retina**: The inner layer of the eyeball can also be affected.

5. **Symmetrical pattern**: Often, the white patches appear symmetrically on both sides of the body.

If you suspect you or someone you know may have vitiligo, it's important to consult a healthcare provider for an accurate diagnosis and appropriate management.
