  - Maintains scalp health and prevents dryness.
- **Usage**: Can be used daily or as part of a regular hair care routine.

### Key Differences
- **Target Audience**: 
  - Ketovid is for individuals dealing with scalp issues like dandruff or fungal infections.
  - Pristine is for those looking for a regular, possibly more luxurious shampoo for everyday hair care.
- **Ingredients and Focus**:
  - Ketovid focuses on medicinal properties to treat specific scalp conditions.
  - Pristine focuses on enhancing the overall look and feel of hair with possibly natural and nourishing ingredients.

### Choosing the Right Shampoo
