# Yellow Urine After Multivitamin

---
**User:**

Urine is yellowish after taking multivitamin

---
**Assistant:**

The yellowish color of urine after taking multivitamins is typically due to the presence of riboflavin (vitamin B2). Riboflavin is a water-soluble vitamin, and when taken in excess, the body excretes the surplus through urine, which can result in a bright yellow or neon yellow color. This change in urine color is harmless and a common side effect of taking multivitamins.
