Stuttering or low frame rate issues on the LinkedIn app can occur due to several reasons. Here's a list of troubleshooting steps you can try to resolve the issue on your OnePlus 8T:

1. **Update the App**: Ensure that the LinkedIn app is updated to the latest version from the Play Store. Older versions may have bugs causing performance issues.

2. **Clear App Cache and Data**:
   - Go to *Settings > Apps > Manage Apps > LinkedIn*.
   - Tap on *Storage & Cache* and clear both the cache and data (note: clearing data will log you out of the app).

3. **Reinstall the App**: If clearing cache and data doesn't help, try uninstalling and reinstalling the LinkedIn app to refresh its configuration.

4. **Disable Battery Optimization**:
   - Go to *Settings > Battery > Battery Optimization*.
   - Find the LinkedIn app and select *Don’t optimize*. Battery optimization can sometimes restrict app performance.

5. **Update System Software**: Ensure your OnePlus 8T is running the latest available OxygenOS version by going to *Settings > System > System Updates*.

6. **Change Display Settings**:
   - Try switching between 60Hz and 90Hz in *Settings > Display > Screen refresh rate*. Sometimes apps aren’t fully optimized for higher refresh rates.

7. **Check for Background Apps**: LinkedIn might stutter if too many apps are running in the background. Try closing unnecessary apps to free up system resources.

8. **Reset App Preferences**:
   - Go to *Settings > System > Reset Options > Reset App Preferences*. This resets all app preferences but won’t delete any data.
