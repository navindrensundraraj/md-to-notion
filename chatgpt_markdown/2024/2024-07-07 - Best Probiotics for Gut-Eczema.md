### Gut Health
1. **Lactobacillus rhamnosus GG (LGG)**: Known for its ability to survive stomach acid and colonize the gut.
2. **Bifidobacterium longum**: Helps maintain a healthy gut barrier and immune response.
3. **Lactobacillus acidophilus**: Supports digestion and the absorption of nutrients.

### Eczema
1. **Lactobacillus rhamnosus GG (LGG)**: Studies have shown it can help reduce eczema symptoms, especially in children.
2. **Lactobacillus paracasei**: Known for its anti-inflammatory properties.
3. **Bifidobacterium lactis**: Supports skin health and reduces inflammation.
4. **Lactobacillus reuteri**: Can help balance the skin microbiome and reduce eczema symptoms.
5. **Lactobacillus plantarum**: Has anti-inflammatory properties that can help manage eczema.

### Combination and Dosage
A combination of different strains is often more effective than a single strain. The exact dosage and strain combination can vary based on individual needs and should ideally be determined in consultation with a healthcare professional.

### Note
