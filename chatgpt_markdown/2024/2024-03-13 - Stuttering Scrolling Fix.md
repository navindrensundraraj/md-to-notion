# Stuttering Scrolling Fix

---

If the LinkedIn Android app is stuttering when scrolling, try these troubleshooting steps:

1. **Restart the App**: Close the LinkedIn app completely and reopen it.
2. **Restart Your Device**: Sometimes a simple restart can solve performance issues.
3. **Check for Updates**: Make sure you have the latest version of the LinkedIn app installed from the Google Play Store.
4. **Clear Cache**: Go to your device's settings, find the LinkedIn app, and clear its cache.
5. **Check Storage**: Ensure your device has enough free storage space available.
6. **Disable Background Apps**: Close other apps running in the background that might be consuming resources.
7. **Check Network Connection**: A poor network connection can also cause stuttering. Try using LinkedIn on a different Wi-Fi network or switch to mobile data.
8. **Contact LinkedIn Support**: If the issue persists, reach out to LinkedIn support for further assistance or to report the problem.

Hopefully, one of these steps resolves the scrolling stuttering issue in the LinkedIn app on your Android device.
