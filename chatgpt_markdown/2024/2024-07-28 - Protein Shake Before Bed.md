# Protein Shake Before Bed

---
**User:**

Can i drink protein shake at night before sleep

---
**Assistant:**

Yes, you can drink a protein shake at night before sleep. Consuming protein before bed can help with muscle repair and growth, especially if you've had a workout earlier in the day. Casein protein is often recommended for nighttime as it digests slowly, providing a steady release of amino acids throughout the night. However, make sure it fits within your overall dietary goals and doesn't disrupt your sleep.

I'm taking whey protein

---
**Assistant:**

Whey protein is also a good option, even at night. While it's a fast-digesting protein, it can still be beneficial for muscle recovery and growth if you take it before bed. If you're looking for a slower-digesting option, you might consider mixing your whey with some milk, as the casein in milk will slow down the digestion process.
