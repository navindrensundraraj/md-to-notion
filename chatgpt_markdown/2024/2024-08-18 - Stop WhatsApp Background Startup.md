# Stop WhatsApp Background Startup

---
**User:**

how to stop whatsapp from starting in background

---
**Assistant:**

To stop WhatsApp from starting in the background, you can adjust the app's settings on your device. Here's how you can do it depending on your device type:

### **For Android:**

1. **Disable Background Activity:**
   - Go to **Settings** > **Apps** > **WhatsApp**.
   - Tap on **Battery** or **Battery Usage**.
   - Look for an option like **Background Activity** or **Allow Background Activity** and toggle it off.

2. **Restrict Data Usage:**
   - Go to **Settings** > **Apps** > **WhatsApp**.
   - Tap on **Mobile Data & Wi-Fi** or **Data Usage**.
   - Toggle off **Background Data**.

3. **Force Stop:**
   - Go to **Settings** > **Apps** > **WhatsApp**.
   - Tap on **Force Stop**.

4. **Use Battery Optimization:**
   - Go to **Settings** > **Battery** > **Battery Optimization**.
   - Find **WhatsApp** and set it to **Optimized**.

### **For iPhone (iOS):**

1. **Disable Background App Refresh:**
   - Go to **Settings** > **General** > **Background App Refresh**.
   - Find **WhatsApp** and turn off **Background App Refresh** for it.

2. **Restrict Mobile Data Usage:**
   - Go to **Settings** > **Cellular** or **Mobile Data**.
   - Scroll down to **WhatsApp** and turn off the toggle to restrict it from using cellular data in the background.

3. **Force Quit the App:**
   - Double-click the **Home button** (or swipe up from the bottom of the screen on iPhones without a Home button) to bring up the app switcher.
   - Swipe up on the WhatsApp preview to close it.

4. **Low Power Mode:**
   - Enabling **Low Power Mode** under **Settings** > **Battery** will also prevent WhatsApp from running in the background.

### **General Tips:**
- Avoid opening WhatsApp if you don't want it to run in the background, as simply opening the app will make it start processes.
- Consider disabling notifications if you're not concerned about missing messages.

These steps should help you control when WhatsApp runs and prevent it from starting up in the background automatically.

To stop WhatsApp from starting in the background on Windows, you can follow these steps:

### **1. Disable Startup in Task Manager:**
   - Right-click the **Taskbar** and select **Task Manager**, or press **Ctrl + Shift + Esc** to open it.
   - Click on the **Startup** tab.
   - Find **WhatsApp** in the list of programs.
   - Right-click on it and select **Disable**. This will prevent WhatsApp from starting automatically when you boot up your computer.

### **2. Change WhatsApp Desktop Settings:**
   - Open **WhatsApp Desktop**.
   - Click on the **three dots** (menu icon) in the top left corner.
   - Go to **Settings** > **General**.
   - Uncheck **Start WhatsApp at login**.

### **3. Prevent WhatsApp from Running in the Background (Windows Settings):**
   - Go to **Settings** > **Privacy** > **Background apps**.
   - Scroll down and find **WhatsApp** in the list.
   - Toggle off the switch to prevent WhatsApp from running in the background.

### **4. Close WhatsApp Manually:**
   - If WhatsApp is open, you can close it by right-clicking the **WhatsApp** icon in the system tray (near the clock) and selecting **Quit WhatsApp**.

These steps should stop WhatsApp from running in the background and from starting automatically when your Windows PC boots up.
