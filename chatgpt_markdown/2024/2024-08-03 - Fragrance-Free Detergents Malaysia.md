  1. Grate the Soap: Begin by grating the unscented Castile soap into fine shreds.
  2. Mix the Ingredients: In a large pot, combine the grated soap, washing soda, and borax.
  3. Add Water: Pour hot water into the pot and stir the mixture until all ingredients are dissolved.
